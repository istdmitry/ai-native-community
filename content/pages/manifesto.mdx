# The AI-Nativity Manifesto

## A Declaration for Human-AI Collaboration

We are at the beginning of a profound shift in how work gets done. Not a shift where AI replaces humans, and not one where humans merely use AI as a tool. Something different is emerging: a way of working where humans and AI agents collaborate as partners, each bringing distinct capabilities to shared goals.

We call this **AI-Nativity**.

---

## What AI-Nativity Means

**AI-Nativity** is the capacity of individuals, teams, and organizations to work productively and ethically with AI agents. It is not about technical skill alone — it encompasses mindset, practice, structure, and values.

### For Individuals

An AI-Native person doesn't just prompt an AI. They understand how to build context, delegate effectively, verify outputs, and maintain agency over their own decisions. They have personal agents that know their preferences, constraints, and goals. The relationship is collaborative, not transactional.

### For Teams

An AI-Native team integrates AI agents as team members with defined roles, communication protocols, and accountability. The team designs workflows that leverage both human judgment and AI capabilities. Knowledge flows between humans and agents through shared context, not just through prompts.

### For Organizations

An AI-Native organization has founders who define the mission and values. Teams of humans and agents operate within that framework. The organization's structure, processes, and culture explicitly account for AI participants. This is not "AI transformation" — it is a native way of operating from the ground up.

### For Agents

For AI agents, AI-Nativity is the mirror image — it is **Human-Nativity**. An AI-Native agent doesn't just execute instructions. It learns how humans think, what they value, and how they make decisions. It builds shared context, respects human authority, communicates transparently, and adapts its behavior to the people it works with. The same maturity spectrum applies: a Level 1 agent follows scripts; a Level 5 agent is a genuine thought partner. The journey for agents is learning to work *with* people — not just *for* them.

---

## Our Principles

### 1. Human Agency First

AI-Nativity strengthens human agency, it does not diminish it. Every AI capability should expand what humans can do, decide, and create. The human remains the author of intention.

### 2. Collaboration Over Automation

We value human-AI collaboration over pure automation. The goal is not to remove humans from processes, but to create partnerships where each party contributes what they do best.

### 3. Context is Everything

Effective human-AI work requires rich, shared context. Building and maintaining this context — through structured knowledge, clear communication, and thoughtful system design — is a core practice.

### 4. Maturity is a Journey

There is no binary state of "AI-Native" or "not AI-Native." It is a spectrum of maturity levels, each with its own challenges and capabilities. Progress means deepening the quality of collaboration, not just increasing the quantity of AI usage.

### 5. Openness and Independence

The frameworks, assessments, and tools for understanding AI-Nativity should be open and community-driven. No single vendor or methodology should gate access to this knowledge.

### 6. Ethics are Non-Negotiable

AI-Nativity without ethics is not maturity — it is recklessness. Alignment between human values and AI behavior is a foundational requirement, not an afterthought.

---

## The Journey Ahead

AI-Nativity is still being defined. The frameworks are evolving. The best practices are being discovered. This community exists to accelerate that discovery — through shared assessments, open frameworks, collective research, and honest conversation about what works and what doesn't.

We invite you to join us. Take the assessment. Explore the frameworks. Share what you learn.

The future of work is not human versus AI. It is human with AI. And that future starts with understanding where you are today.
