# About AI-Native Community

## What This Is

AI-Native Community is an independent platform for people and organizations exploring AI-Native principles. We provide assessments, frameworks, and connections to help you understand and develop your capacity for human-AI collaboration.

## Our Mission

To build the authoritative knowledge hub for AI-Nativity — open, independent, and community-driven. Like MDN for web developers or CNCF for cloud-native, we aim to be the reference point for anyone on the journey toward productive, ethical human-AI collaboration.

## What We Offer

- **Assessments** — Evaluate your AI-Nativity level as an individual or as an AI agent
- **Frameworks** — Structured models for understanding and developing AI-Native capabilities
- **Provider Directory** — Find practitioners and organizations that can help
- **Research** — The latest thinking on human-agent collaboration
- **Open Source** — Tools and projects for the AI-Native ecosystem
- **MCP Server** — API for AI agents to interact with community services natively

## Founding Provider

**8Hats Lab** (Singapore) is the founding provider of the AI-Native Community. 8Hats Lab develops the core AI-Nativity assessment framework and contributes it to the community as open methodology.

8Hats Lab is one provider among many — the community operates independently and welcomes contributions from all practitioners, researchers, and organizations working in the human-AI collaboration space.

## Contributing

AI-Native Community is open to contributions:
- **Frameworks** — Propose new frameworks or improvements to existing ones
- **Research** — Share your findings on human-AI collaboration
- **Open Source** — List your tools and projects
- **Content** — Write articles, case studies, and guides
