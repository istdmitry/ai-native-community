# HALA Framework

**Human-AI Learning Architecture**

HALA is a framework for designing learning experiences that build AI-Native capabilities. Developed by 8Hats Lab, it provides a structured approach to helping individuals and teams advance their AI-Nativity levels.

## Core Principles

### 1. Learn With AI, Not About AI
Traditional AI training teaches people about AI capabilities. HALA designs learning experiences where humans and AI learn to work together through practice.

### 2. Progressive Complexity
Learning experiences are structured to match the learner's current AI-Nativity level, gradually increasing the complexity and autonomy of human-AI collaboration.

### 3. Reflection and Feedback
Each learning cycle includes structured reflection on the quality of human-AI collaboration, not just the output quality.

## Architecture Layers

### Layer A — Principles
The foundational beliefs and values that guide human-AI collaboration. Includes ethical frameworks, collaboration principles, and quality standards.

### Layer B — Practices
Concrete skills and workflows for effective human-AI work. Includes prompting, context building, delegation, evaluation, and coordination.

### Layer C — Projects
Applied learning through real projects that require human-AI collaboration. Each project is designed to exercise specific practices at the learner's level.

## Application

HALA is used by providers in the AI-Native Community to design coaching programs, workshops, and learning paths. If you're a provider interested in using HALA, check the [Provider Directory](/directory) or reach out through the community.
