# AI-Nativity Levels Framework

The AI-Nativity Levels framework measures how effectively individuals, teams, and organizations collaborate with AI agents. It provides a shared vocabulary for understanding where you are and where you can grow.

## The Six Levels

### L1 — Explorer
You're aware of AI and have tried basic tools. Interaction is transactional — you prompt, AI responds. There's no persistent context or ongoing relationship with AI agents.

### L2 — Practitioner
You use AI regularly in your work. You've developed basic prompting skills and understand that context matters. You're building initial habits around AI collaboration but still treat AI as a tool, not a partner.

### L3 — Integrator
AI is woven into your daily workflows. You maintain context across sessions, delegate routine tasks effectively, and can evaluate AI output quality. The relationship is becoming collaborative.

### L4 — Architect
You design systems and workflows that explicitly leverage human-AI collaboration. You understand the strengths and limitations of both parties and create structures that maximize joint effectiveness.

### L5 — Orchestrator
You coordinate multiple AI agents alongside human team members. You've developed communication protocols, accountability frameworks, and quality assurance processes for multi-agent environments.

### L6 — Visionary
You're shaping the future of human-AI work. You contribute to frameworks, develop new methodologies, and help others advance on the journey. You understand AI-Nativity at organizational scale.

## Five Pillars

Each level is assessed across five pillars that represent different dimensions of AI-Native capability:

1. **Context System** — How you build and maintain shared context with AI
2. **Delegation & Trust** — How effectively you delegate to and trust AI agents
3. **Evaluation & Quality** — How you assess and ensure AI output quality
4. **Collaboration Patterns** — How you structure human-AI interaction
5. **Strategic Integration** — How AI fits into your broader goals and strategy

## Assessment

The assessment evaluates your level across all five pillars using scenario-based questions. Your overall level is determined by the weakest pillar — reflecting the principle that AI-Nativity requires balanced capability across all dimensions.
